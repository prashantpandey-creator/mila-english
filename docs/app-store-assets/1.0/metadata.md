## Description

Mila helps Russian-speaking learners practise real English without pressure.

Speak naturally. Mila transcribes your answer, replies like a patient tutor,
and reads the response aloud. Use short conversations to prepare for travel,
work, interviews, and daily life.

Learn in the way that suits the moment:

- practise a spoken conversation with Mila;
- chat about grammar or role-play everyday situations;
- review practical vocabulary and pocket phrases;
- take a complete level check;
- follow your lesson and pronunciation progress;
- continue as a private guest without creating an account.

Privacy is part of the product. Mila has no advertising or cross-app tracking.
Voice recordings are sent only after you tap the microphone, used to create a
transcript, and deleted after the request. You can permanently delete your
guest profile or registered account from inside the app.

Some learning modules require an internet connection. Pocket phrases remain
available in the native app when the network is unavailable.

## Review notes

Mila is fully usable without credentials. On first launch the app creates an
isolated guest profile automatically. The reviewer can open Learn, Speak, Mila,
and Progress from the native tab bar.

To test the tutor, open **Mila** and tap a suggested conversation. A private
model cold start can occasionally take up to three minutes before the first
reply. To test voice, open **Speak**, allow microphone access, tap the large
microphone, say one sentence, and tap it again. The app submits that recording
for transcription, produces a tutor reply, and plays speech audio.

There are no purchases, subscriptions, ads, or tracking in version 1.0. Account
deletion is available from the profile icon on Home. The complete level check,
listening, and grammar modules open focused first-party web views from
`mila.purangpt.com`; the core app surfaces are native SwiftUI.

## App Privacy declaration

No data is used for tracking and no data is used for third-party advertising.
The following data is linked to the learner and used only for App Functionality:

- Name (only when the learner registers)
- Email Address (registered email or internal guest placeholder)
- User ID (internal account or guest identifier)
- Other User Content (chat messages and retained voice transcripts)
- Product Interaction (lesson, vocabulary, assessment, and progress activity)

Microphone audio is transmitted only to service the learner's explicit
transcription request and is deleted after the request. It is not retained or
used for any secondary purpose, so it is treated as transient request data
rather than collected App Privacy data.

## Export compliance
